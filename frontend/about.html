About Ashgrtz

More Than Apparel. It's A Mindset.

Born From The Grind

Ashgrtz was forged in the fire of countless training sessions, born from a simple need: apparel that could keep up. Tired of gear that sacrificed style for performance, or performance for style, we set out to create something different.

Our mission is to provide elite-level fitness apparel that not only withstands your most brutal workouts but also looks sharp enough to wear beyond the gym walls. We believe that what you wear is an extension of your dedication.

Our Core Principles

Performance-Driven Design

Every seam, fabric, and fit is meticulously engineered to enhance movement, provide support, and optimize your performance.

Uncompromising Quality

We source only premium, durable materials that are sweat-wicking, comfortable, and built to last, session after session.

Modern Aesthetic

We combine minimalist design with bold accents to create a look that is powerful, modern, and unmistakably Ashgrtz.
